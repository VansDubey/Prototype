About Us

Scanned Kheti is a revolutionary platform dedicated to helping farmers overcome challenges and maximize their crop yield. Our mission is to provide comprehensive support and resources to farmers, addressing issues such as seed quality, weather conditions, lack of assistance, and community support.

Through our platform, farmers can access a range of services including location-based crop recommendations, soil testing, crop planning, agricultural tools rental, mandi rate updates, and a digital dialing system for non-smartphone users.
